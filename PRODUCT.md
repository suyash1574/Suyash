## Brand Personality
Playful, interactive, high-tech, and engaging. It utilizes smooth animations, custom hover effects, particles/interactive elements, and vibrant color gradients (deep blues, purples, and electric accents) to create a premium digital experience.

## Anti-references
- The standard boring, static grid template.
- Plain SaaS-style landing pages (beige backgrounds, static cards with simple icons).
- Overly cluttered, slow-loading pages with excessive, meaningless decorations.

## Design Principles
1. **Interactive Delight**: Every section should feature a subtle hover or click feedback element that makes the website feel responsive and alive.
2. **Showcase by Doing**: Highlight complex engineering achievements (like the AI Interview System) with visual highlights and interactive components rather than just plain text paragraphs.
3. **Typographic Authority**: Use clean, modern sans-serif typography with generous spacing and balanced layouts to ensure readability and professional impact.
4. **Vibrant Depth**: Employ rich color gradients and custom shadows instead of solid flat colors to create a modern, premium feel.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios meeting WCAG AA standard (>= 4.5:1 for body text).
- Responsive resizing for different device viewports (desktop, tablet, mobile).
- Alt tags on all images/visuals and descriptive labels on buttons/inputs.
- A `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` media query rule to disable complex particle effects/animations for users who prefer reduced motion.
